Privacy Policy

Effective: May 2026

This is a small app. The privacy of the cup matters as much as the reading itself. The shorter version of this page: we collect what we need to give you a reading and remember it later, and nothing else.

What we collect

When you sign in with Apple, Google, or your email, we receive your email address from that provider. That email becomes your account identifier and is the only personally-identifiable thing we store about you.

When you take a reading, you upload three photos of your cup. We send those photos to Anthropic's Claude API, which generates the reading text. The photos themselves are not retained after the reading is generated. Only the resulting reading text is stored, alongside your account.

How we store it

Your readings are stored in a Supabase Postgres database. Row-level security ensures one user can never see another user's readings. Data at rest is encrypted by Supabase. Connections are HTTPS-only.

Third parties

The app uses three third-party services:

  • Apple, Google, Email magic link for sign-in. We receive only your email and a stable user identifier.
  • Anthropic Claude API to generate readings from your photos. Claude does not retain the photos after responding.
  • Supabase to store your account and readings.

We do not use advertising networks, analytics platforms, or trackers of any kind. There are no cookies on this site.

AI processing & consent

When you take a reading, the app asks for your explicit permission before sending your cup photos to Anthropic's Claude API. No photos leave your device until you consent. This consent is requested each time you initiate a reading. You may decline at any point, though a reading cannot be generated without it.

Data retention

Your readings are kept for as long as your account exists. If you delete your account, all associated readings and your email address are permanently removed from our database within 30 days. Photos of your cup are not retained at all — they are sent to the Claude API in memory only and discarded once the reading text is returned.

Your rights

You can sign out from the Settings tab in the app at any time. You can delete your account directly from the Settings tab, or by emailing hello@anushcoffee.com. In either case, all your data is deleted within 30 days.

California residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect and how we use it, the right to request deletion of your data, and the right to not be discriminated against for exercising these rights. We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. To exercise any of these rights, email hello@anushcoffee.com.

European residents (GDPR)

If you reside in the European Economic Area, our legal basis for processing your email address is the performance of a contract — providing you the service you signed up for. Our legal basis for processing your cup photos is your explicit consent, given each time you initiate a reading. You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, to restrict or object to processing, and to data portability. To exercise any of these rights, email hello@anushcoffee.com.

Data controller

Anush is developed and operated by Vardan Kirakosyan, based in the State of California, USA. For all privacy-related inquiries, contact hello@anushcoffee.com.

Children

Anush is not designed for children. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes

If we ever change this policy meaningfully, we will note the change at the top of this page with a new effective date. The older versions will remain accessible.
